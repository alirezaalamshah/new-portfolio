This tool automates the modem-update process end to end: it connects to modems over Telnet and downloads and installs update files through a TFTP server.

The goal is to simplify and speed up modem updates in high-volume work environments — and like the other production-line tools, it is designed for users with no computer expertise.

Full details are in the project brief (PDF).
